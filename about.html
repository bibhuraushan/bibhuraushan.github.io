About Me

A brief overview of my research interests and work.

Bio

I am a scientist working in solar physics, with a focus on solar magnetic fields, synoptic data products, and data-driven modeling. My work emphasizes reproducible scientific pipelines using Python and Fortran.

Research Interests

  • Solar magnetograms (LOS & vector)
  • Synoptic map construction and resampling
  • Surface Flux Transport models
  • Polar field evolution
  • AI/ML-based instrument cross-calibration

Tools & Skills

Python Fortran NumPy Pandas HDF5 FITS Git Linux / macOS

Current Focus

Developing robust, physically consistent pipelines for full-disk and synoptic solar magnetic field data, with an emphasis on long-term datasets.
